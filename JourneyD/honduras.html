Discover the best hotels in Honduras

Copantl Hotel & Convention Center Honduras

Copantl Hotel in San Pedro Sula features 191 rooms ranging from standard to presidential suites. Amenities include a gym, swimming pool, tennis courts, laundry services, and a large convention center with 19 event rooms accommodating up to 5,000 guests, plus a helipad and spacious parking.


InterContinental Real San Pedro Sula Honduras

This luxury hotel from the InterContinental chain provides high-end lodging in San Pedro Sula. It’s perfect for business travelers, with modern rooms, fine dining, a spa, business center, gym, and pool—offering five-star comfort and service.


Las Cascadas Lodge Honduras

Las Cascadas Lodge is a boutique all-inclusive eco-lodge in the Honduran rainforest. Each room overlooks a private natural waterfall. The property spans over 100 hectares of forest and offers hiking, waterfall rappelling, spa, sauna, jacuzzi, and gourmet meals by private chefs.


Indura Beach & Golf Resort Honduras

Located in Tela, this luxury beachfront resort blends nature and comfort. It features tropical villas, a spa, fitness center, beach club, 18-hole golf course, tennis courts, and multiple pools. Perfect for relaxation or outdoor adventures along the Caribbean coast.
