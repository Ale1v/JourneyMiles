Discover the best hotels in El Salvador

Hotel Miramonte El Salvador

Hotel Miramonte in San Salvador offers a comfortable and centrally located stay ideal for travelers seeking great value. With cozy rooms, breakfast included, and a swimming pool, it’s located in the Zona Rosa near shopping centers, restaurants, and nightlife. A friendly, family-style atmosphere makes it suitable for both tourists and business trips.


Hotel Villaserena El Salvador

Villaserena Hotel is a modern boutique hotel in a quiet area of San Salvador. It features spacious and elegant rooms, a gym, spa, swimming pool, and an in-house restaurant. Its location near the hotel district and major shopping areas makes it an attractive option for guests seeking comfort and quality service.


Hotel Grecia Real El Salvador

Grecia Real is a 3-star hotel located in the Real area of San Salvador, near Metrocentro. With approximately 50 rooms, free breakfast buffet, swimming pool, and complimentary Wi-Fi, it is ideal for budget travelers looking for safety, good service, and proximity to commercial zones.


Royal Decameron Salinitas El Salvador

Royal Decameron Salinitas is an all-inclusive resort on El Salvador’s Pacific coast, located in Sonsonate, about 50 minutes from San Salvador. It offers 552 rooms, over 1 km of private beach, saltwater and freshwater pools, six bars, multiple buffet and à la carte restaurants, tennis courts, nightly shows, and unlimited activities.
