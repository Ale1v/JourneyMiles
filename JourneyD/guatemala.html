Discover the best hotels in Guatemala

Porta Hotel Antigua Guatemala

Porta Hotel Antigua offers a charming colonial experience just minutes from Antigua Guatemala’s main square. Surrounded by gardens and local birds, this hotel features traditional architecture, an outdoor pool, restaurant, spa, and beautifully decorated rooms with Guatemalan flair.


AC Hotel by Marriott Guatemala City

Located in Ciudad Cayalá (Zone 16), this stylish Marriott hotel offers a contemporary stay with buffet breakfast, fitness center, rooftop bar, and direct access to upscale shops and restaurants. Ideal for travelers seeking modern comfort close to the airport.


Tikal Futura Hotel & Convention Center Guatemala

Tikal Futura is a modern hotel and convention center in Guatemala City, integrated into a commercial complex. It has 205 rooms, conference spaces for over 6,000 people, a restaurant, spa, gym, swimming pools, and design elements inspired by Mayan culture and modern architecture.


GHL Hotels Guatemala Guatemala

With properties in Guatemala City and beyond, GHL Hotels offers modern rooms, meeting spaces, restaurants, and a strategic urban location. Ideal for business travelers and groups looking for functional and well-equipped accommodations.
