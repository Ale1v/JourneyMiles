Guatemala

You can visit Tikal, an impressive Mayan archaeological site, or explore Lake Atitlán, surrounded by picturesque towns and volcanoes. You can also enjoy the nightlife and gastronomy of Guatemala City, or visit local markets filled with handicrafts and color.

El Salvador

In El Salvador, you can enjoy a variety of activities, from exploring Mayan archaeological sites to relaxing on volcanic beaches and enjoying nature in national parks. You can also delight in the rich local culture and cuisine, including the famous pupusas.

Nicaragua

In Nicaragua, you can enjoy a variety of activities, from exploring colonial cities and volcanoes to relaxing on beaches and practicing extreme sports. You can also immerse yourself in the local culture through its markets, handicrafts, and delicious cuisine.

Costa Rica

In Costa Rica, you can enjoy a wide variety of activities, from exploring volcanoes and cloud forests to relaxing on idyllic beaches and practicing water sports. You can also immerse yourself in the local culture by visiting museums, markets, and participating in festivals.

Honduras

In Honduras, there is a wide variety of activities to enjoy, from exploring Mayan archaeological sites to relaxing on idyllic beaches and enjoying nature. Some popular options include visiting the Copán ruins, diving in the Bay Islands, exploring Pico Bonito National Park, and enjoying the beaches of Roatán.

Panamá

Some popular options include visiting the Panama Canal, exploring Casco Viejo, relaxing on paradisiacal beaches like Bocas del Toro or San Blas, or venturing into outdoor activities like hiking in national parks or diving on Coiba Island.
