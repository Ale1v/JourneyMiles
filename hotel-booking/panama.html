Discover the best hotels in Panama

Waldorf Astoria Panamá

Waldorf Astoria Panama offers a luxury experience in the heart of Panama City. Located steps from the Cinta Costera and the financial district, the hotel features elegant rooms, gourmet dining, a spa, pool, and exceptional service. Its proximity to Casco Viejo and the vibrant nightlife of Calle Uruguay makes it the ideal choice for both business and leisure travelers.


Megapolis Hotel Panama

Megapolis Hotel Panama offers a modern and vibrant experience in the heart of the city. Located next to Avenida Balboa and across from the Cinta Costera, the hotel features spacious rooms with panoramic views, international dining, a spa, pool, and live entertainment. Its proximity to shopping centers, the financial district, and nightlife makes it an excellent choice for both business and leisure travelers.


Hilton Panamá

Hilton Panama combines comfort, luxury, and a prime location on Avenida Balboa, offering stunning views of the Pacific Ocean. The hotel features modern rooms, fine dining, a spa, fitness center, casino, and outdoor pool. Its proximity to the financial district, the Cinta Costera, and Casco Viejo makes it an excellent choice for both business and leisure travelers.


Riu Plaza Panama

Riu Plaza Panama offers a modern and urban experience in the heart of Panama City’s financial district. Featuring stylish rooms, buffet and international restaurants, a business center, gym, spa, and outdoor pool, the hotel provides everything for a comfortable stay. Its prime location makes it ideal for both corporate travelers and tourists.
