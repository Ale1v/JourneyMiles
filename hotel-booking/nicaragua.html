Discover the best hotels in Nicaragua

Hotel Nicté

Hotel Nicté blends elegance and serenity in a welcoming atmosphere. With comfortable rooms, a restaurant, swimming pool, and lush surroundings, it’s ideal for relaxation and disconnecting. Its location makes it perfect for romantic getaways or family vacations.


Hotel Contempo Managua

Hotel Contempo is a luxury boutique hotel located in a quiet and exclusive area of Managua. It blends contemporary design with personalized service, featuring elegant rooms, a gourmet restaurant, spa, gardens, and event spaces. Ideal for travelers seeking comfort, privacy, and a refined experience in Nicaragua’s capital.


voco Lake Nicaragua – Granada

voco Lake Nicaragua – Granada is a modern hotel offering distinctive style and premium comfort. Located by the lake and near the historic center, it features elegant rooms, signature dining, a spa, pool, and authentic experiences. It’s the perfect mix of relaxed luxury and local culture.


Globales Camino Real

Globales Camino Real is a top-category hotel located near Managua’s International Airport. It offers spacious rooms, an international restaurant, gym, pool, sports courts, and a convention center. It's ideal for both business travelers and tourists seeking comfort and convenience.
