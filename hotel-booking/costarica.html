Discover the best hotels in Costa Rica

Hotel Presidente

Hotel Presidente is a stylish boutique hotel located in downtown San José, perfect for exploring the city. It features comfortable rooms, complimentary breakfast, a restaurant serving local cuisine, a gym, and a rooftop terrace with panoramic views. Its prime location makes it ideal for travelers seeking culture, food, and urban experiences.


Radisson Hotel San José

Radisson Hotel San José offers comfort and quality service in a quiet area near the city center. The hotel features spacious rooms, a restaurant, pool, fitness center, business facilities, and meeting rooms. It’s a great choice for both business travelers and tourists visiting San José.


Hotel Adventure Inn

Adventure Inn is a friendly, eco-conscious hotel located near Juan Santamaría International Airport. It offers spacious rooms, free breakfast, a restaurant, pool, gym, and custom tour services. It's an excellent choice for travelers starting or ending their Costa Rican adventure.


Hotel Samara Beach

Just steps from the ocean, Hotel Samara Beach is a charming hotel in the heart of Playa Sámara. It offers comfortable rooms, complimentary breakfast, a tropical garden, pool, and easy access to the beach, restaurants, and water activities. Perfect for a relaxing beach getaway.
